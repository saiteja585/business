About Us

Welcome to Swagruha Organic Foods – your one-stop destination for authentic, homemade Telangana foods. We offer a wide variety of traditional Veg Pickles, Non-Veg Pickles, and delicious Snacks — all made with love, care, and premium organic ingredients.

From spicy Mango, Lemon, and Gongura pickles to savory Chicken and Mutton pickles, and crunchy delights like Chekkalu, Gavvalu, Karapusa, and Laddu — every item reflects the rich culinary heritage of Telugu households.

Our mission is to bring the true taste of Telangana tradition to your plate, one jar or snack at a time. We maintain high standards of hygiene and quality, ensuring every bite is pure, flavorful, and memorable.
